Top 5 Paborito In Tarlac City

by Kazuki Kawasaki

1. Victor's - Inihaw na Tilapia

I love to use my hands on eating this food. Adding soy sauce with crushed chili on the side will even make this food way yummier

2. Bok's Chicken - Soy Garlic Boneless Chicken

They have the best korean fried chicken. Their boneless chicken is rich in flavor even without bones

3. Chong's Inasal - Chicken Inasal

Best Chicken Inasal and Cajun Seafood in town. You won't get disappointed.

4. Yoo Ja Ne - Samgyup Set Meal with complete Side Dishes

Not eat-all-you-can samgyupsal but the quality is really good. I love to takeout from this restaurant cuz their side dishes are well prepared.

5. Tessie's - Sisig

It is a original kapampangan style Sisig. No mayo or egg. Just a simple chopped grilled pork with lots of diced onion on top. Simply the best sisig in town.
